Mapping Health Data

This project maps racial population percentages and infant mortality rates by county across South Carolina using U.S. Census and SC DHEC data.

Tools Used

ArcGIS Dot Density Mapping Choropleth Mapping Inset Map Design Table Joins Legend Conversion

Key Insights

  • Racial percentages vary significantly by county, particularly for Black or African American populations.
  • Infant mortality rates are elevated in several rural counties.
  • Dot density maps provide spatial intuition for total population.
  • Choropleth maps reveal normalized disparities more effectively.
  • Custom legend formatting and inset mapping enhance clarity.

Included Maps

  • Infant Mortality Rates (2004–2008)
